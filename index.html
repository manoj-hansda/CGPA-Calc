Formula Used

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is calculated as:

            C1G1 + C2G2 + C3G3 + --- + CnGn
    CGPA = --------------------------------
            C1 + C2 + C3 + --------- + Cn
where G1, G2 --- denote the grade point scored, and C1, C2 --- denote the credits of subjects.
All theory Subjects, Lab and Seminar work Carry 4 Credits. Project work Carries 8 Credits.

CGPA is converted into percentage with a multiplier of 9.5.

Grades and its corresponding grade points are given below:

MarksGradeGrade Point
>=80A+10

60 to 65B7

<40FAIL--

The final result will indicate the division/class as under:

CGPA of 9 or moreDistinction

CGPA 5 or more but less than 6.5Second Division
Less than 5FAIL (No award will be given)
